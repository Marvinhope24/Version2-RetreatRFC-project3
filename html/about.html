About Us

Retreat RFC

Our rugby club is based in the Southern Suburbs of Retreat, Cape Town, South Africa. Our club is affiliated to Western Province R.F.U. and we are currently competing against the Southern regions' biggest clubs.

Our aim is to eradicate crime, while giving our children of Retreat a platform to showcase their skills. Our main concern is to keep them off of the streets, and this is where you come in play. We kindly invite you to join our loving community, as your support and help is highly needed to boast their spirits.

To our community, we would like to thank you for always being our biggest fans and being part of this precious and hard-working club. We want you to know our appreciation for your support and we will make sure we bring success to our community, as long as you keep supporting our boys!
